About

EGoPart Ltd. was founded as a medicinal herbs and medical devices supplier. At EGoPart Ltd. we understand the importance of what we’re doing. We know that the healthcare market is moving away from “one size fits all” products and we’re committed to being a part of creating personalized care. Towards that goal we design, market proprietary, disposable medical and medicinal products.

We’re here to help advance the practice of medicine and we are serving millions of people in this effort. We are dedicated to help people fight serious illnesses and promoting an environmentally friendly option.

Our flagship products, consists of a full line of products that meet the needs of healthcare professionals, self-administration users and pharmaceutical companies.
